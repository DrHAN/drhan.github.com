    About Me

    My name is Bo Han (韩波), and you can call me Kevin. I am now a research assistant at underwater vehicle laboratory (UVL), headed by Professor Bo He, Ocean University of China. My research interests lie on the interface of machine learning, cognitive science and computer vision. Currently, I am working on two fantastic topics: 1) Sparse Representation and Dictionary Learning 2) Brain-Inspired Neural Networks.
