My research interests lie on the interface of machine learning, cognitive science and computer vision.     Research Experience

    Sparse Representation and Dictionary Learning:
1 Ongoing: I am working on a topic related to robust face recognition via sparse
representation previously proposed by Prof. Yi Ma.

2 Oct.2013-Jan.2014: I expanded a project called Computational Models of Early 
Visual Cortex previously led by Dr.Shan (Gary's group, UCSD). The new model well 
mimic the functional roles of early visual layers like V1 or V2.

Brain-Inspired Learning Algorithms:
1 Ongoing: I am also working on a topic related to convolutional neural networks 
(CNN) previously proposed by Prof. Yann Lecun.

2 Jun.2012-June.2014: I finished a project called Ensemble Learning based on Extreme
Learning Machine which includes two new frameworks. The first one is the LARSEN-ELM 
employing the LARS algorithm. And the other is the RMSE-ELM based on recursive 
models.

    Recently, I pay more efforts on sparse representation for fast face recognition via different regularization and bio-inspired feature extraction.
